About staff

Fuel Equipment Improver (FEI) is a highly efficient additive added to engine fuel to improve the health and lifespan of the engine. FEI is especially made to produce lower frictive, smoother mechanical surfaces without leaving behind a film deposit. For high-pressure fuel pumps or injector nozzles, whether a part is new or refurbished, a single application of FEI prior to installation is sufficient. The FEI provided a projected payback period that is less than a year.

Key benefits of using FEI include

Avoiding wear, seizures, and damage to moving parts

Decrease in leaks

Increased equipment lifespan

Improved fuel oil atomization
