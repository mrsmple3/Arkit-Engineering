About

Under the Akrit Engineering, we specialize in producing computerized products for diesel engines and boilers. Our patented technology is built to give you the best outcomes possible. With the help of our products, customers can maximize fuel and power efficiency while lowering maintenance and testing requirements.

Akrit Engineering offers a wide selection to meet all of your equipment needs, ranging from engine pressure indicators, crankshaft deflection monitors, and portable diagnostic sets to fuel-saving units, additives for better lube economy, and high-pressure fuel equipment.
