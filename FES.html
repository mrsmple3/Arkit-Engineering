About staff

A highly efficient solution that works for both boilers and engines is called Fuel Eco Saver (FES). FES is a cutting-edge fuel oil treatment that works as a carbon catalyst, smoke suppressant, and combustion enhancer for two- and four-stroke engines as well as boilers. Heavy fuel and diesel both work well with it. FES is installed on a fuel line before engine or boiler. It drastically cuts fuel oil consumption by 5 to 10%. For newer engines in good condition, using FES can result in fuel savings of about 5%, while older engines frequently see even greater improvements. The typical fuel economy gain for boilers is around 10%. FES not only reduces fuel consumption but also emissions (CO and NOx) by about 15%, which lowers maintenance and component costs by at least 15% . The projected payback period for FES is less than a year, which represents a fantastic return on investment.

Reduced unburned fuel and improved combustion efficiency.
