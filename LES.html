About staff

Lubrication Eco Saver (LES) is an additive to the lube oil which renders mechanical surfaces even smoother and less friction without depositing a film. LES is suitable for engines, compressors and equipment using gears and bearings. LES saves fuel oil, lubricating oil and also improve upon emissions. It is very important that the mechanical surface has no film deposited upon it, but consists instead of a highly-polished metal. The engine requires treatment only once and additional treatment is not required, except when replacing major components, such as the pistons or cylinder liners or changing oil. In the case of oil change extra 10% of initial quantity LES should be added. The treatment is effective for two- and four-stroke diesel engines, compressors, hydraulic systems and gearboxes, as well as in equipment with rolling and plain bearings. LES offers a projected payback period of less than one year
